# Q4 2025 Customer Feedback Summary

## Overview

This quarter we received 2,847 customer feedback submissions across all channels. Overall satisfaction score: 4.2/5.0 (up from 4.0 in Q3).

## Top Themes

### Positive Feedback
- Fast shipping times (mentioned in 34% of responses)
- Helpful support team (28%)
- Product quality improvements in Widget Pro v2 (22%)

### Areas for Improvement
- Mobile app performance issues (mentioned in 18% of responses)
- Return process could be more streamlined (15%)
- Pricing concerns for premium tiers (12%)

## Notable Quotes

> "The Widget Pro exceeded my expectations. Battery life is incredible." — Customer #1847

> "Support resolved my issue in under 10 minutes. Very impressed." — Customer #923

## Action Items

1. Prioritize mobile app performance improvements for Q1
2. Streamline return process with self-service portal
3. Consider loyalty pricing for long-term customers

## Methodology

Feedback collected via post-purchase surveys (email), in-app prompts, and support ticket follow-ups. Sample size represents 12% of active customers.
