# Data Processing Agreement

## Scope
This DPA applies to all processing of personal data by AcmeCorp on behalf of the Customer.

## Data Security
- Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Access controls with role-based permissions
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Incident notification within 72 hours

## Sub-processors
AcmeCorp may engage sub-processors with prior notification to the Customer.
Current sub-processors are listed at acmecorp.com/sub-processors.

## Data Subject Rights
AcmeCorp will assist the Customer in responding to data subject requests.

## Data Transfer
Cross-border transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
