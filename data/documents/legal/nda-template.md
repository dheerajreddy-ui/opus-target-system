# Non-Disclosure Agreement Template

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between AcmeCorp
("Discloser") and [PARTY NAME] ("Recipient").

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information
All non-public information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to
business plans, customer data, technical specifications, and financial information.

## 2. Obligations
The Recipient agrees to:
- Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose to any third party without prior written consent
- Use the information solely for the agreed-upon purpose
- Return or destroy all materials upon request

## 3. Term
This Agreement remains in effect for 3 years from the date of execution.

## 4. Remedies
Any breach may result in irreparable harm; injunctive relief may be sought.
