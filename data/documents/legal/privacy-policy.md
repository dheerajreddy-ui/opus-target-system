# AcmeCorp Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## Information We Collect
- Account information (name, email, phone)
- Payment information (processed securely, not stored)
- Order history and preferences
- Usage data and analytics

## How We Use Your Information
- Process orders and provide customer support
- Improve our products and services
- Send order updates and promotional materials (with consent)
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Order history: 7 years for tax compliance
- Usage analytics: 2 years
- Support conversations: 1 year

## Your Rights
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Data portability
