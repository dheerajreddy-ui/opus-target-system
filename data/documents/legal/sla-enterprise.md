# Enterprise Service Level Agreement

## Service Availability
- Guaranteed uptime: 99.9%
- Scheduled maintenance: Max 4 hours/month with 48-hour notice
- Emergency maintenance: As needed with best-effort notice

## Response Times
| Severity | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Critical | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| High | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| Medium | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| Low | 8 hours | 72 hours |

## Support
- 24/7 phone and email support for Critical/High
- Business hours support for Medium/Low
- Dedicated account manager
- Quarterly business reviews
