# DMCA Notice and Takedown Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-01-01
**Version:** 1.1

## Filing a Notice
To report copyright infringement, send a written notice to dmca@acmecorp.com with: identification of the copyrighted work, URL of infringing content, your contact information, and a statement of good faith.

## Counter-Notification
If you believe content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification. AcmeCorp will restore content within 10-14 business days unless the complainant files a court action.
