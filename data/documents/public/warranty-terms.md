# AcmeCorp Warranty Terms

## Standard Warranty
All AcmeCorp products come with a 1-year standard warranty covering manufacturing defects.

## Extended Warranty
- Available for purchase within 30 days of original purchase
- Extends coverage to 3 years
- Covers accidental damage (1 claim per year)
- Cost: 15% of original product price

## What's Covered
- Hardware defects
- Battery degradation (below 80% capacity)
- Screen defects
- Component failures

## What's Not Covered
- Physical damage from drops or misuse
- Water damage (unless device is rated water-resistant)
- Unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Normal wear and tear
