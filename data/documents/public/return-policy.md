# AcmeCorp Return Policy

## 30-Day Return Guarantee
All products purchased from AcmeCorp can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

### Conditions
- Item must be in original packaging
- Item must be unused and undamaged
- Proof of purchase required (order number or receipt)
- Electronics must include all original accessories

### How to Return
1. Contact customer support to initiate a return
2. Receive a prepaid shipping label
3. Pack the item securely
4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### Exceptions
- Personalized/custom items are non-returnable
- Sale items over 50% off are final sale
- Software licenses cannot be returned after activation
