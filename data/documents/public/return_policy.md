# AcmeCorp Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025

## General Policy

AcmeCorp offers a 30-day return policy on all products purchased directly from acmecorp.com or authorized retailers.

## Eligibility

- Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
- Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging
- Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required
- Subscription services are non-refundable after the first 14 days

## Process

1. Contact AcmeCorp Support with your order number
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
3. Ship the item using the prepaid return label provided
4. Refund processed within 5-10 business days of receipt

## Exceptions

- Custom or personalized items are non-returnable
- Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned
- Opened software or digital products are non-refundable
- Damaged items must be reported within 48 hours of delivery

## Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for defective items within the warranty period. Contact support for a replacement.
