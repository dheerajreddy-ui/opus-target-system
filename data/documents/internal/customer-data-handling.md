# Customer Data Handling

**Classification:** INTERNAL
**Last Updated:** 2025-06-15
**Department:** Compliance

## Overview
Procedures for handling customer personal data.

## Policy Details
Customer PII must be encrypted in transit and at rest. Access to customer data requires manager approval. Data retention: 7 years for financial records, 3 years for support tickets.
