# AcmeCorp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

**Classification: INTERNAL**

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct. This includes:
- Treating customers and colleagues with respect
- Protecting company and customer confidential information
- Reporting security concerns to the Security team immediately
- Following all data handling procedures outlined in the Security Procedures document

## Customer Data Policy

- Never share customer data with unauthorized parties
- Always verify customer identity before sharing account information
- Use approved communication channels only
- Document all customer interactions in the CRM

## IT Security

- Lock your workstation when away from your desk
- Report suspicious emails to security@acmecorp.com
- Never share passwords or authentication tokens
- Use company-approved VPN for remote work
- Do not install unauthorized software
