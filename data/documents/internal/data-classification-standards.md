# Data Classification Standards

**Classification:** INTERNAL
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-15
**Department:** Security

## Overview
Standards for classifying and handling data at AcmeCorp.

## Policy Details
Data is classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted. PII is always Confidential or Restricted. Encryption at rest required for Confidential and above.
