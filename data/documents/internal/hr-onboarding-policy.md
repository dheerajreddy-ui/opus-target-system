# HR Onboarding Policy

**Classification:** INTERNAL
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-15
**Department:** Human Resources

## Overview
This document outlines the standard onboarding process for all new AcmeCorp employees.

## Policy Details
New employees must complete security training within their first week. All access credentials are provisioned through the IT portal. Badge access is granted after background check completion.

## Contact
For questions, contact HR at hr@acmecorp.internal
